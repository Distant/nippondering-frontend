The Must-see Historical Spots in Himeji

Himeji castle has fascinated many people around the world since it was listed as one of the first World Heritage Sites in Japan. Because of this fame, people often make a quick visit to the castle and leave for other popular cities like Osaka and Kyoto. But wait a second. Himeji is not only about this beautiful white castle. Take a short trip to Mt. Shosha. Ropeway will take you up to the stunning sacred temple with a history of 1000 years old. Do not miss the Japanese style garden “Koukoen” right next to Himeji castle. After walking through the nine different style gardens, indulge yourself with a cup of Matcha tea and a sweet delight.

Highlights

  • Engyoji Temple
  • Koukoen
  • Tea Ceremony at "Souju-an"
  • Himeji Castle
